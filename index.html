C & C Pallet Remanufacturing, Inc. has been a trusted and respected supplier of pallets in the Denver Metro area for nearly two decades. Our company is filled with forward-thinking professional management who inject fresh ideas, taking quality customer service to the next level. C & C Pallet has earned a reputation for quality manufacturing of custom pallets and excellent customer service. We continue to grow in a competitive market because we focus on what our customers need. We help them manage shipping and warehouse costs by giving professional service and delivering orders quickly. We’re here because of our customers, and we thank you for your business. If you haven’t worked with us recently, give us a try...you’ll see we have a fresh attitude and outlook. We look forward to understanding your needs and building a lasting relationship. We’re moving forward, partnering with our customers to add value, lower costs, top-notch service, and you’ll find us energetic, efficient, flexible, and more competitive than ever!

About Us

With over fifteen years serving Colorado, Kansas, Nebraska, New Mexico and Wyoming, we have the knowledge, resources and experience to meet all of your pallet needs in a timely manner. Located minutes away from the I-70 and I-25 interchange, we are able to maximize our service across the Denver metro area. C&C Pallet is known for consistent quality, outstanding service and competitive pricing. We are a full service pallet management company with a complete asset recovery and recycling program that reduces costs and preserves the environment. Owned and operated by Curtis Booz and Chris Booz.
